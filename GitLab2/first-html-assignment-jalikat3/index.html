A Bit About Me

By Jali Purcell

My full name is Jali Katherine Purcell. My name is a combination of my dad's name, Jason, and my mom's name, Lisa. Katherine is a family name. I was born in Springfield, Missouri, and I live in Republic, Missouri. I go to school at Drury University, majoring in Computer Science: Software Engineering and Spanish, with a minor in Cyber Risk Management.

Besides taking classes, you can see me on campus playing the bass drum in the Drury Panthers Marching Band, or filming videos as a DUTV staff member. I also enjoy reading, writing, and watching movies with friends. My core values are learning and adaptability. I feel most in my element when I am learning something new, or advancing my skills with something I already know. I am not afraid of big life changes, and look forward to whatever new changes come my way every day.

I chose this picture because it shows that I like to travel. This picture was taken in the Grand Canyon this summer. I am very lucky in that our family is in a position to take frequent family trips every year. We have taken family trips to Colarado, Wicsonsin, California, Florida, and this last one, Arizona to Utah, ending in Las Vegas, Nevada. I have been out of the United States to Mexico and Canada, on the way to Alaska on a cruise. I hope to continue my love for travel in my adult life, starting with studying abroad in Spain in Summer 2022.
